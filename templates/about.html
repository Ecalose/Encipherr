What is Encipherr ? :

Encipherr is a web application that offers you a powerful encryption of texts or files.


How it works ? :

This app uses Advanced Encryption Standard(AES);It is a symmetric Encryption algorithm that use a random generated key to encrypt and decrypt whatever data is being encrypted/decrypted.
