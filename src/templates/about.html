About Encipherr


Encipherr is an online tool that provides a simple way to protect sensitive data with a powerful encryption algorithm.

Encipherr uses AES which is considered one of the strongest algorithms available.

The encryption process is fast, and the website itself is lightweight and easy to use.

It's fast and free and open sourceâ€”and there are no ads!

Encipherr offers no tracking mechanisms. This helps to keep your privacy safe while browsing on any device or computer.


How to use Encipherr ?

Text encryption

Enter your AES key in the key box. Then click Encrypt. To decrypt data, The same process as the encryption.

File encryption

Enter your AES key in the key box, Select your file, Then click Upload and Encrypt, The same process for the decryption.

Important !

If you want to read your own encrypted data, you'll need to have the same key that was used in the encryption.
So, when you lose or forget the encryption key for a file or text, it is impossible to recover it.
